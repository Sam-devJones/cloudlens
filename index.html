What is CloudLens?

    Smart Glasses

Technologies Used:

A High powered Processor and Graphical Processing Unit capable of Handling Demanding 4k Rendering and Augmented Reality.

Components like Intel Xeon processor and AMD Radeon graphics.

Regular Prescription glasses with a Simple CPU mounted on the rim capable of data transmission and a Network Adaptor to access Internet.

Every Product has its Pros & Cons

    Pros

  • No Wired Connections required.
  • Transparent lens and advanced sensors helps Designers and Manufactures perfect their product easily.
  • Light weight ,easily portable.
  • AR made useful and engaging world experiences.

    Cons

  • Stable Internet Connection is always required.
  • Currently suitable for Industries and NOT COMMERCIAL USE.
  • Might make User vulnerable to Eye Strains and Headaches.
